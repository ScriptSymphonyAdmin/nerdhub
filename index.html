Apps & Games

Atlas Arcanum

browser map & game-master tool Original Creation

A browser tool for running sandbox campaigns without a tower of books at the table. Outline the regions of a map you already have, then tuck everything behind it — area descriptions, private notes, and encounter tables tuned to the terrain and your party’s level. Flip to Play mode, click a region, and roll. It speaks both D&D 5e and Old-School Essentials, ships with dozens of original encounter and event tables, generates new ones on demand, and lets you build your own in seconds — all on a single page, nothing to install or sync.

Open the app

Middle Sea NPC Generator

browser toolkit for The Middle Sea Original Creation

A browser toolkit for the Middle Sea setting, bundling four tools in a single file. The NPC Generator builds complete stat blocks across the setting’s 55 classes — ability scores with racial modifiers, HP, AC, THAC0, saves, spells, proficiencies, languages, and every class-specific quirk (dragon bloodlines, mutations, mental powers, lycanthrope forms, Artificer gadgets, and more), with race/class restrictions and level caps enforced. A Mass Combat builder computes unit type, quality, and points of advantage; a Spell Lists reference covers every casting class plus the full Artificer gadget catalogue; and an Equipment browser holds all 71 weapons, 17 armour types, and 12 shields. Runs from one HTML file — no install, no connection, no dependencies.
